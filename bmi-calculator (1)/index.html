What is BMI? Body Mass Index (BMI) estimates body fat based on height and weight. While it’s a helpful screening tool, it doesn’t account for muscle mass, bone density, or distribution of fat.

Healthy Habits: Aim for balanced meals, regular exercise, quality sleep, and hydration. Consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice—especially if your BMI is outside the normal range.
